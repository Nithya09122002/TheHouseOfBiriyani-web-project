    • Our Story

    Our Biryani Heritage

    Founded in 1995, The House of Biryani began as a small family-owned restaurant in Hyderabad, passionate about preserving the authentic art of biryani-making. What started as a humble kitchen serving traditional Hyderabadi dum biryani has now grown into a beloved institution with multiple locations across India, each committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and authenticity.

    Our secret lies in the perfect harmony of premium aged basmati rice, carefully selected spices ground fresh daily, and the slow dum pukht cooking technique that allows flavors to mature beautifully. Each biryani is prepared with the same love and attention to detail that our founders instilled in their original recipes. From the royal kitchens of the Nizams to the bustling streets of Kolkata, we bring you the diverse flavors of India's rich biryani heritage in every aromatic mouthful.

    Traditional Recipes

    We use century-old recipes passed down through generations of master chefs, ensuring each biryani maintains its authentic taste and preparation methods exactly as created in royal kitchens.

    Premium Ingredients

    Only the finest Dehraduni basmati rice, farm-fresh meats, and hand-picked spices go into our biryanis. We source ingredients directly from trusted suppliers who share our quality standards.

    Dum Pukht Technique

    Our biryanis are slow-cooked using the traditional dum method, where the pot is sealed with dough to trap aromatic flavors and allow the meat to tenderize perfectly in its own juices.

    Meet Our Master Biryani Chefs

    Chef Rizwan Ahmed

    Head Biryani Master

    With over 30 years of experience, Chef Rizwan specializes in Hyderabadi and Lucknowi biryanis. Trained under the legendary Ustad Habib Pasha, he brings royal kitchen secrets to every preparation, ensuring perfect rice texture and spice balance.

    Chef Priya Sharma

    Vegetarian Specialist

    Chef Priya creates exquisite vegetarian biryanis that even meat-lovers crave. Her signature paneer biryani and kathal (jackfruit) biryani have won numerous awards. She combines traditional techniques with innovative ingredients.

    Chef Arfat Khan

    Regional Varieties Expert

    Chef Arfat has mastered regional biryani styles across India. From the subtle flavors of Kolkata biryani to the fiery Chettinad version, he ensures each variety maintains its distinctive character while meeting our quality standards.

    Chef Ramesh Patel

    Spice Master

    With an encyclopedic knowledge of spices, Chef Ramesh creates our signature spice blends. He personally selects and roasts whole spices for optimal flavor. His expertise ensures consistent taste in every batch of biryani we serve.

    About The House of Biryani

    Since 1995, we've been serving authentic biryanis prepared with traditional recipes and the finest ingredients. Our passion for perfecting this royal dish has made us one of India's most beloved biryani destinations, with multiple outlets across the country.
